Our Services

We can even customize consulting services based on your dating needs. We also work with you one on one to find your best matches. Register to be a member and contact us today!

Becoming a member

Membership

Join other singles in Kansas City and become a member of Status Dating! Status Dating memberships are for singles that are serious about dating and relationships. All STATUS members are local to the Kansas City area. They go through a screening process with a background check to ensure there are no fake profiles, and no shady backgrounds. We use a local and personal approach to matching singles.

How it works

It’s simple! To become a member sign up below. Choose a membership package then fill out a quick questionnaire and our dating consultants will notify you of your membership status and our consultants will contact you. You can also contact- us directly by phone or email to make an appointment with one of our dating consultants.

Criteria

Memberships are only for those that meet STATUS requirements and pass our special screening process. We look for single members that can pass a background check and have an average or better professional and/or social status in Kansas City.

Membership Benefits

Members attend our singles events for free, have access to use our consulting services, and are able to use our private database to search and connect with other members!
