Our Services

Events

Our singles events are top notch! Our fun interactive singles events are for Status members and non-members over the age of 21 to meet other local singles. While attending our events you can also find out more about our company and decide if you would like to become a member. We will have a variety of different singles events around different parts of the city each month. They include entertainment, food, and drinks. Join our email list to be notified of when and where our events will be.

Consulting

We offer dating consulting services by appointment for Status screened members only. You will be able to meet with one of our dating consultants and pick from our different consulting services packages. We work with you one on one to find your best matches. As a member, you can customize your own private singles events and we will recruit members specifically for you to meet. We also offer dating advice, coaching, date planning, and lifestyle/fashion advice from experts included in your membership!

Search

Our search database is for local Status screened members only! It will allow you to build a profile to use to connect with other local screened members and will include different features that allow you to choose how you how you want to search and be found! We also offer many fun features to make your on-line dating experience easy and fun.
