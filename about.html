Who We Are

Real People, Real Dates, Real Relationships

Don’t expect the typical dating service where you never know who you are really talking to or if members even have real profiles. What you can expect is a company with real people, real dates, and real relationships! We connect our members with other quality members through our events, consulting services, or our website database. We use our special screening process to make sure each member is properly vetted with a background check and proper status in Kansas City to keep all of our members local! What do we mean by status? Well, to become a member you will have to pass our background check and then we check your professional status and social status to decide if you can become a member. We have come up with this process for a safer way to date and for quality members that we know are serious about finding a match. Don’t worry, if you don’t want to use our membership services then you can still attend our singles events that take place around the city on a regular basis. Feel free to join our email list for us to notify you of when our next event will be!
