Who We Are

A Dating Service Kansas City Needs!

Don’t expect the typical dating service where you never know who you are really talking to or if members even have real profiles. What you can expect is a company with real people, real dates, and real relationships on a local level! We connect our members with other quality members through our events, consulting services, or our website database for Kansas City area singles only. We use our special screening process to make sure each member is properly vetted with a background check and proper status in Kansas City to keep all of our members local! What do we mean by status? Well, to become a member you will have to pass our background check and then we check your professional status and social status to dedetermine membership membership. We have come up with this process for a safer way to date and for quality members that we know are serious about finding a match. Don’t worry, if you don’t want to become a member then you can still attend our singles events. Feel free to join our email list for us to notify you of when our next event will be!

Mission Statement

Our purpose is to connect like-minded, screened, and background checked individuals for safer better quality dating. We provide members with real people. real dates, and real relationships. We strive to give piece of mind about the dating process while still making it exciting and fun. We provide the best consulting resources to personally introduce you to a status match for one- on-one dates, as well as fun quality singles events and a website with a local database for personal interaction and searches.
